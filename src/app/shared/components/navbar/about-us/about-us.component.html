🤝 About Smart Supply

Empowering Supermarkets with Direct Factory Access — Fast, Reliable & Digital.

What is Smart Supply?

Smart Supply is a web-based platform that connects supermarket owners directly with factories producing household and grocery products. Instead of phone calls or middlemen, store managers can place orders digitally and track deliveries in real-time.

Whether you're managing one store or a chain of branches, Smart Supply helps you save time, reduce costs, and restock your shelves with confidence.

🎯 Our Mission

To simplify and digitize the supply chain between manufacturers and retailers — making product ordering, tracking, and fulfillment seamless.

🚀 Our Vision

We aim to become Egypt’s leading B2B marketplace for supermarket supply, driving innovation in logistics, inventory, and supplier relationships.

✅ Why Choose Smart Supply?

  • 📦 Direct ordering from factory without middlemen
  • 🚚 Real-time order tracking and delivery updates
  • 🧾 Invoice history and order management
  • 💬 Easy communication with admins via in-app chat
  • 🔐 Secure user roles (Admin / Customer) with email confirmation
